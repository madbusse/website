About Me

I'm Madeline, and I'm restless. Whether I'm channeling my energy into a sunrise run or a coding project, I'm constantly chasing something new and exciting - but I'll always make time to pause and enjoy a good cup of coffee.

I am a huge runner and have done two marathons so far! Next on my list is the 2026 Austin Marathon.

I love coffee culture and worked as a barista all four years of college. Cafes are great third spaces and I love the community they create.

My favorite place to be is on a hike. It's a goal of mine to through-hike the Appalachian Trail, but for now I enjoy squeezing in quick backpacking trips whenever I can.
